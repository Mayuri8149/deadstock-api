Sourcing

Sourced directly from the heart of “Sierras Subbéticas Natural Park”, Priego de Cordoba in Andalusia, Spain

Location: Donlope Servicios Generales, C/ Llana Nº 5 , El Cañuelo. Priego de Córdoba 14815, Spain

Andalusia's geography and climate, gifted with mountain wealthy slopes, mild winters and long and hot dry summers, rank as the best for olive cultivation. Olives from this region are destined exclusively for olive oil production.

This region is located in the geographical heart of the “Sierras Subbéticas Natural Park”, which has its own microclimate, characterized by suitable rainfall and substantial temperature differences between seasons. It can drop to around 0º C (32º F) in winter and rise to 35º C (95º F) in warm western Mediterranean summer.

Production

GRUPO DON LOPE only elaborates extra virgin olive oil obtained from the fruit of olive trees of varieties Picudo, Hojiblanco and Picual, cold extracted and processed by mechanical means that cause no alteration in the oil, retaining the flavour, aroma and characteristics of the olives from which it is obtained.

Quality

Produced under the Organic Agriculture certificate using exclusively organic fertilizers and minimum tillage to respect, to the maximum, the flora and fauna of the tree's soil and the Environment.

100% Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil (Olive Juice) with CAAE ECOLOGICAL CERTIFICATE and ES-ECO-001-AN EU AGRICULTURE seal

Acidity is the basic and simplest indicator of the quality of virgin olive oil. Don Lope Olive oil has a top value in this quality parameter 0.2%. Virgin olive oils must not exceed an acidity value of 2%. The limit for extra virgin olive oils is 0.8%
